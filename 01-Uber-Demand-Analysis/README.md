# Uber Demand Analysis

## Project Overview

This project analyzes Uber pickup demand patterns across New York City using historical ride, weather, and temporal data. The objective is to identify factors influencing ride demand and provide data-driven recommendations for operational optimization.

---

## Business Objective

As a Data Scientist supporting Uber's NYC operations, the goal is to:

- Identify demand patterns across time periods
- Understand the impact of weather conditions on ride volume
- Evaluate location-based demand differences
- Generate actionable recommendations for resource allocation

---

- Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)

---

## Key Insights

- Demand varies significantly by time of day.
- Weather conditions influence ride frequency.
- Certain boroughs generate substantially higher ride demand.
- Peak demand periods provide opportunities for optimized driver allocation.

---

## Business Recommendations

- Increase driver availability during peak demand windows.
- Utilize weather forecasting for operational planning.
- Focus incentives on high-demand locations.
- Improve rider experience through predictive demand management.
