## Project Overview

This project analyzes customer ordering behavior, restaurant performance, cuisine demand, delivery operations, and customer ratings for FoodHub, a food delivery platform operating in New York City.

---

## Business Objective

The objective is to identify trends in customer demand and operational performance that can help FoodHub improve customer satisfaction, delivery efficiency, and restaurant partnerships.

## Key Insights

- American and Japanese cuisines generate the highest demand.
- Weekend ordering activity significantly exceeds weekday activity.
- Customer satisfaction is generally high among submitted ratings.
- A small number of restaurants drive a large percentage of orders.

---

## Business Recommendations

- Strengthen partnerships with top-performing restaurants.
- Increase customer participation in ratings and reviews.
- Optimize delivery operations during peak demand periods.
- Promote high-value orders through targeted marketing.
